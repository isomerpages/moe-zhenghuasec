<h2>General Learning Routines with PLD</h2>
<h4>General Precautions</h4>
<p>1. Ensure the working area is free of food and drinks.
<br>2. Ensure that PLD is always placed on a level surface.
<br>3. Always carry your PLD with closed lid.
<br>4. Do not lean on, poke or put pressure on the screen.
<br>5. Do not place anything (e.g. pens, pencils, notebooks) on the keyboard
before closing the lid.
<br>6. Ensure that the identification label provided by the school is placed
on PLD lid. If your label is torn, please approach General Office for assistance.
<br>7. Keep your PLD in locker when not in use and lock your locker at all
times.</p>
<h4>Before Leaving Home</h4>
<p>1. Charge your device daily / bring a fully charged device to school.
<br>2. Ensure that the stylus is inserted in storage port.
<br>3. Put your PLD in its protective case.
<br>4. Place your PLD, mouse and earpiece into your bag.</p>
<h4>Before Morning Assembly</h4>
<p>1. Turn on your PLD and check that it can connect to school WIFI (pdlp@SSOE).
<br>2. Shut down your PLD.
<br>3. Safekeep your PLD inside your bag or locker. Never leave your PLD unattended.
<br>4. Switch off your mobile phone and keep it in locker.</p>
<h4>During Lesson (Require PLD)</h4>
<p>1. Place your PLD flat on top right corner of your table at all times.
<br>2. Never leave your PLD unattended.
<br>3. Place your screen at least 45 degree downwards when your teacher is
giving instructions or teaching.
<br>4. Use only functions or programs permitted by teacher.
<br>5. Mute your PLD unless permission is obtained from the teacher for instructional
purposes.
<br>6. Save your work regularly and 5 minutes before the end of the lesson.
<br>7. Shut down and keep your PLD in locker at the end of lesson.
<br>** When going to special rooms i.e. Art Room, D&amp;T and Science lab,
carry your PLD with the lid closed</p>
<h4>During PE</h4>
<p>1. Ensure your PLD is shut down.
<br>2. Safekeep your PLD in your locker.</p>
<h4>During recess or lunch break</h4>
<p>1. If you are using PLD, never leave your PLD unattended.
<br>2. Students are strongly encouraged to use the electronic devices for
learning. (Refer to Acceptable Use Policy)</p>
<h4>During CCA or Activities</h4>
<p>1. Safekeep your PLD in your locker.
<br>2. If you are required to use PLD, never leave your PLD unattended.</p>
<h4>Before Leaving School</h4>
<p>1. Check that the stylus is in storage port.
<br>2. Keep your PLD and ear piece in your bag.
<br>3. Bring your PLD back home.</p>
<h4>Technical Issues</h4>
<p>If you encounter any problem during curriculum period,
<br>1. check with your peers or teacher for help,
<br>2. if unresolved, restart your PLD,
<br>3. if it is still unresolved, your teacher will contact ICT personnel.
<br>
<br>If you encounter any problem during non-curriculum time,
<br>1. restart your PLD,
<br>2. if unresolved, bring your PLD to computer lab 1, daily from 2.30 PM
to 3.30 PM.</p>
