<h5>About the Parent Support Group</h5>
<table style="minWidth: 25px">
<colgroup>
<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><em>Formed in 2003, the Zhenghua Secondary School Parent Support Group (ZSS PSG) stands as a strong pillar of partnership between home and school — united by a shared commitment to every child’s growth and success.</em>
</p>
<p><em>The PSG is more than a group of volunteers; it is a vibrant community of dedicated parents who journey alongside the school, supporting programmes and initiatives that nurture not only students’ academic progress, but also their well-being and character.</em>
</p>
<p><em>Through thoughtfully curated workshops and engaging activities throughout the year, the PSG creates meaningful opportunities for families to connect. These moments strengthen parent-child relationships, build a supportive network among parents, and foster the sharing of insights, experiences, and encouragement.</em>
</p>
<p><em>Together, the ZSS PSG continues to shape a caring and connected community — where every student is supported, every parent is empowered, and every journey is shared.</em>
</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<p>How to Join?</p>
<p>Through active participation in the PSG, you will gain support from other
parents in your parenting journey. If you are interested to sign up as
a parent-volunteer, or participate in the upcoming activities organized
by the PSG, you can submit your interest through our online registration
form at <em><a href="https://go.gov.sg/zssparents2026" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/zssparents2026</a></em>
</p>
