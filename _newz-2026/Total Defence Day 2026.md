<p>On 13 February 2026, our school commemorated Total Defence Day with a
Forum Theatre assembly programme on digital disruption. Through engaging
performances and audience participation, students explored the impact of
cyber threats and learnt how to stay vigilant and responsible online.</p>
<p>Suhaifiq Bin Suhaidi (4E)
<br>I learnt that Total Defence is not only about soldiers defending our country.
When I saw the Forum Theatre characters train in bootcamp and handle emergencies,
I realised that students like us also have a role in keeping Singapore
safe and prepared.</p>
<p>Lee Jaimee (4F)
<br>The part about supporting the hawkers and celebrating different cultures
taught me that unity and social harmony are important. I understood that
respecting others and helping local businesses can strengthen our nation.</p>
<p>Yong Wei Qian Belia (4G)
<br>I was most impacted by the digital world scene about online misinformation.
I learnt to think carefully before sharing information and to stay emotionally
strong, because psychological and digital defence are just as important
as physical defence.</p>
