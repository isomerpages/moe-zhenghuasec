<p>From January 7 to 9, our school welcomed our newest batch of Secondary
1 students with open arms during our highly anticipated orientation programme,
Camp Synergy. The name of the camp was chosen with a clear and powerful
purpose: to mark a fresh beginning as they learn to embrace challenges
and work together to become bigger than themselves. Ultimately, the concept
of "Synergy" reflects the core objective of fostering a strong sense of
class unity and teamwork through collaboration and shared experiences.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The undisputed highlight of the orientation was our spectacular campfire
on 9 January. As our first campfire in three years, it served as a brilliant
camp finale.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We are incredibly proud of our incoming cohort and our tireless student
leaders.&nbsp;</p>
<p>And here’s the warmest welcome to our Secondary 1s!</p>
