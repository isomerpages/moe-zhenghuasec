### Mission

To create a fun, varied and nurturing MTL learning environment so as to instil the passion, competence and pride in the language and culture.

### Vision

To groom integrated & informed thinker who is an effective communicator of his Mother Tongue, connected to the world and rooted in Singapore culture.
