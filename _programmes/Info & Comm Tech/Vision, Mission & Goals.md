### Vision
To harness ICT productively for quality learning and to develop students to be future-ready and responsible digital learners.

### Mission
To use ICT effectively to deepen school ICT practices and to develop learners to be **Collaborative, Problem-Solving, Innovative and Creative, Inquiring Mind, Responsible,  Adaptable and Technologically Competent**
