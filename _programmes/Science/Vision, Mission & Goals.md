### Vision

The Science Department envisages our students to become confident individuals with an inquiring mind in this evolving technological world. They will be equipped with knowledge and skills which will enable them to deeply appreciate and understand the world around them so that they can contribute positively to society.

### Mission

To emphasize the importance of critical thinking and logical reasoning in learning science so that students are able to understand the practical applications of science and its significance in context.
