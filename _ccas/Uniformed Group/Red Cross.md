<p>The Zhenghua Secondary School Red Cross Unit is a vibrant co-curricular
activity (CCA) that equips students with essential lifesaving skills and
a strong sense of service. Through this programme, students are trained
to administer first aid and provide critical support during school sports
events, helping to ensure the safety and well-being of all participants.
Beyond technical knowledge, students also develop confidence, responsibility,
and teamwork. Signature activities include First Aid drills, scenario-based
simulations, and community outreach. By actively contributing to school
and public events, our cadets gain valuable real-world experience in managing
emergencies and reducing injury risks, preparing them to be caring and
competent members of society.</p>
