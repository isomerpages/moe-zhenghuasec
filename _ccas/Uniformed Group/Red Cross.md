<p>Zhenghua Red Cross Youth is pleased to be awarded the Gold Excellence
Unit Award (EUA) for the sixth consecutive year. This award recognises
the outstanding efforts of our cadets, volunteer instructors and youth
officers. Our cadets’ interest and commitment propel them to attain accreditation
badges in the various modules offered such as First Aid, Disaster Management
and Youth Ambassadors’ Blood Programme. Contrary to popular belief, Red
Cross cadets do not just focus on first aid training; they are also given
ample opportunities to hone their leadership skills as well as forge friendship
with their peers from other units through HQ-run activities like the Red
Cross Youth Challenge and Warrant Officers’ Programme. It is with great
hope that throughout their years in Zhenghua, all Red Cross Youth cadets
will be able to cultivate the core Red Cross values of compassion, passion
and professionalism for the service of mankind.</p>
